## Advanced Troubleshooting

### "GASTON isn't finding patterns I expect to see"
- **Data Requirements:** Ensure sufficient spatial complexity and sample size
- **Preprocessing:** Verify appropriate feature selection and normalization
- **Parameters:** Experiment with neural network architecture settings
- **Validation:** Compare with traditional methods for sanity checking

### "RNA velocity results seem noisy"
- **Data Quality:** Assess splicing read coverage and gene selection
- **Smoothing:** Apply appropriate spatial and temporal smoothing
- **Validation:** Cross-reference with known biological processes
- **Integration:** Combine with other temporal analysis methods

### "Multi-modal integration shows batch effects"
- **Technical Variation:** Apply stronger batch correction methods
- **Biological Variation:** Distinguish technical from biological differences  
- **Reference Quality:** Evaluate reference dataset appropriateness
- **Validation:** Use orthogonal methods to confirm integration quality
